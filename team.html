Management Team:

Our management team which is led by an Executive chairman is an add-mixture of south Korean and Nigerian top managers who have extensive and in-depth cognate experiences in the automotive filter industry and also in the other manufacturing industries both within and outside the country and this explains why one of the key objectives of the management has been to standardize the industrial operations of Filterland so that it will continue to be in line with the international standards.



The Executive Chairman, SIR BENSON C. ABALOGU is a business guru who supervises a business empire that includes Filterland industries limited etc. His story is like that of Fables Midas who changes everything and anything he comes in touch with into Gold. He is an entrepreneur par excellent and a business czar. He can be described as one of the geniuses of modern business. The managing Director, MR H. K. Lee is a south Korean National. He has more than 30 years experience in the Automotive Filter business including more than 20 years in management. The Group General Manager, MR EMEKA CHUKWURAH is the immediate past General Manager of Air Liquide Nigeria Plc, a multinational company that operates in more than 70 countries with over 36,000 employers world-wide and global annual turnover of 11 billion Euros in countries in 2007. He was on the management of Air liquide for 20 years. Mr Chukwurah has a wealth of management knowledge and experience having been Deputy Director, Corporate Planning & Strategies at consultserve Ltd Victoria Island; General Manager at Banex Industries Ltd, Isolo; Branch Manager at Minolta/UBM Limited Ikeja respectively prior to joining Air Liquide. He has an MBA from the City University, London and a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of East London. He majored in Mathematical, Econometric and Statistical Methods.
